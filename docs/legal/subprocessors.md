# Anthrasite Subprocessors

**Last Updated**: 2025-10-14

Anthrasite uses a number of third-party service providers (subprocessors) to provide our services. These subprocessors may have access to customer data as part of their service delivery. We have vetted each subprocessor for their security and privacy practices.

| Vendor                                 | Purpose                      | Data Type               | Location |
| :------------------------------------- | :--------------------------- | :---------------------- | :------- |
| **Stripe, Inc.**                       | Payment Processing           | Customer & Payment Info | US       |
| **Supabase, Inc.**                     | Database & Authentication    | Lead & User Data, Logs  | US       |
| **Vercel Inc.**                        | Website Hosting & CDN        | Website Traffic Data    | US       |
| **DocRaptor**                          | PDF Report Generation        | Report Content          | US       |
| **PostHog, Inc.**                      | Product Analytics (Optional) | Usage Events            | US       |
| **Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.)** | Error Monitoring & Tracking  | Diagnostic Logs         | US       |
